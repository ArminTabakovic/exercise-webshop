Data Collection on our Website

Contact Form

When you request an Academia license via our contact form, we collect your email address. This data is used exclusively to process your request and to verify your academic status. The legal basis for this is Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.
We store the email address for the duration of the license validity and delete it immeadiately after.

Payment Processing

For the purchase of Enterprise licenses, we use external payment service providers. When you click the purchase button, you are redirected to the provider's website. We do not store or process your credit card information. However, for contract fulfillment, the provider transmits your contact details back to us. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.
The email address is stored for the duration of the license validity and deleted immeadiately after. The contract data is stored for 7 years due to Austrian law (Bundesabgabenordnung § 132).

Server Log Files

The hosting provider (GitHub Pages) states that when a GitHub Pages site is visited, the visitor's IP address is logged and stored for security purposes (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

Cookies & Analysis Tools

We do not use cookies that require your consent. We do not use third-party analysis tools. Any cookies used are strictly necessary for the technical functioning of the site.

Your Rights

You generally have the right to access (Art. 15 GDPR), rectification (Art. 16 GDPR), erasure (Art. 17 GDPR), restriction (Art. 18 GDPR), data portability (Art. 20 GDPR), objection (Art. 21 GDPR). If you believe that the processing of your data violates data protection law or that your data protection rights have otherwise been infringed, you may file a complaint with the supervisory authority. In Austria, this is the Data Protection Authority.
