About us - MINOPTAR

MINOPTAR GmbH is a Vienna-based startup specializing in mathematical optimization.

Trademark Portfolio

All trademarks listed below are owned and created by MINOPTAR GmbH and are registered to protect our brand identity.

1. Word Mark: "MINOPTAR"

The name is reflecting our DNA:

  • MIN stands for Minimization, a core objective in mathematical optimization.
  • OPT represents Optimization, our primary field of expertise.
  • AR and MIN are a tribute to our founder, Armin.
Nizza Classes: 9 (Downloadable software applications for computers) & 42 (Software as a Service (SaaS)), as "MINOPTAR" appears in our software application but is also the brand name for our services.

2. Figurative Mark: The Polyhedron next to MINOPTAR in the header

Our logo is a stylized dodecahedron. In mathematical optimization, business decisions are often modeled as Integer Linear Programs. The set of feasible solutions to these problems is a polyhedron defined by linear constraints. Our logo visually represents the "feasible region" where the optimal solution is found.

Nizza Classes: 9 & 42, as it appears in our software application and is a identity for our services.

3. Movement Mark: The Rotating Dodecahedron

Description: A regular dodecahedron with regular pentagons as faces. The faces are slightly transparent. It rotates 360 degrees over 3 seconds. Colors: Faces: #4fc3c3, Edges: #1a3d3d. This animated logo appears specifically on the loading screen of our software.

Nizza Class: 9 (Downloadable software applications for computers), as it appears in our software application on the loading screen.
